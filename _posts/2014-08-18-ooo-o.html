No rebuttal opportunity, no co-operation

Our Reference: L30/13/1772 18 August 2014 Mr Mel McWeeney Dublin Road Co Longford Dear Mr McWeeney

I refer to your previous correspondence in connection with your appeal of this Office's decision not to uphold your complaint against Longford County Council.

In line with the Office's appeals procedure, I have asked Mr. Sean Garvey, Senior Investigator, to examine your appeal. It is expected that your appeal will be completed within 4-6 weeks.

If it appears, however, that the appeal will take longer than expected, Mr. Garvey will advise you accordingly.
