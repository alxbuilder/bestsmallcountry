ODPC - Ombudsman investigation or examinations excempt

Dear Mr McWeeney

I refer to your recent email to this Office.

A very small number of agencies are not required to deal with access requests under the Data Protection Acts. The Office of the Ombudsman is one of those organisations. While we do not have sight of the response that you have received from The Office of the Ombudsman, there is a legal basis which provides that they do not have to provide access to information which they have obtained in the course of an investigation or examination.

Accordingly it would appear that this Office is not in a position to investigate this matter on your behalf.
